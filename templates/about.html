Who We Are

AetherCare: Transforming Healthcare

AetherCare is an innovative healthcare platform developed by three B.Tech CSE students from ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad, as part of their final-year project (Batch 2025). Our mission is to harness generative AI to make healthcare more accessible, accurate, and personalized. From AI-powered symptom prediction to real-time hospital finding, we’re building a one-stop solution to empower individuals in their health journey.
